Welcome To The Official Website Of Pensarora

On this website you will find all of the information you need about Pensarora. This site has information on everything in Pensarora from government to what you will find in the museums.

What Is Pensarora?

Pensarora is a community concept built for the twenty-first century, not just stuck in it. Pensarora's goals are to solve many of the problems communities face today. We challenge preconceived opinions to help citizens become more open to change.

What To See In Pensarora

While in Pensarora, explore the history of this ancient land and water through the canals. Walk, run, hike or explore our famous central park. If looking to tour the city on wheels, take one of our free bikes out to get around fast, interestingly and without increasing greenhouse gasses.

Mission

Pensarora aims to surpass the best of twenty-first century life and set the standard for new communities. On top of this, Pensarora would like to become a community in which citizens welcome change in their everyday lives and where cultural biases are nonexistent.

Location

Pensarora will be located at 44.5075° N, 11.3514° E, the existing site of Bologna, Italy. Centrally located in Europe, it allows easy access to Asia and Africa. Like its more well known sister city Venice, Bologna has a 60 Km network of covered over and mostly forgotten canals. The repurposing of these canals serves as a model for change and repurposing in communities.

Pensarora

Pensarora comes from the Italian Pensare Ora, meaning Think Now. Pens, citizens of Pensarora pride themselves on their ability to live thoughtfully in the moment while also striving for a better future.
